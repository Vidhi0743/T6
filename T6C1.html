Clue 2: A riddle that holds the key to an enigmatic concept.

Decipher this cryptic clue to unlock an elusive concept, often hidden in plain sight:

“I am the key to a boundless mystery, a creation born not from nature, but from the mind. I twist and bend in harmony with the universe, yet no one can touch me. What am I?”

Hint: It's a concept related to geometry and the universe.

Correct! The answer is the "Golden Ratio" (also known as Phi or φ), a mathematical constant that appears in nature, art, and architecture.
